A Real Space

A Real Space is a space for urban education, critique, discussion, and empowerment. It is a space for the city, in the city. Our aim is to facilitate critical inquiry and catalyse informed debate; to be a place where the public and the private, the local and the global, the radical and the conventional, the theoretical and the practical, can come together, learn, unlearn, and wrestle with how we want to live together in our cities. See our Agenda for more details.

Visit us

Located in the heart of Helsinki, atop the Punavuori district, A Real Space is open to the public between 13:00-18:00 (Mon-Thu) and 10:00-18:00 (Fri). Find us on Google Maps.
